Registering Post Graduate level courses
Undergraduate students having a CGPA of 8.00 or more are permitted to credit a Post
Graduate level course, in his/her major area of specialization (or related discipline), against
his/her UE option only. An undergraduate student is permitted to register a Post Graduate
level course as an audit course only, if his/her CGPA is equal to or more than 8.00 at the
time of Registration. However, no relaxation of conditions indicated in Sections 6.2 and 6.3 is
permitted while auditing/ crediting Post Graduate level courses
